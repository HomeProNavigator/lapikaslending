Featured Service Areas Across Southern Nevada

Derek Lapikas serves buyers and homeowners throughout the Las Vegas Valley and surrounding communities. Here’s a quick overview of the areas we specialize in.

Henderson

Henderson is Southern Nevada’s largest suburb and one of the most popular areas for families and professionals. It offers a strong mix of master-planned communities, excellent schools, and convenient access to both the Las Vegas Strip and quieter residential neighborhoods.

Learn more about Henderson →

Summerlin

Summerlin is one of the most desirable master-planned communities in the U.S., located at the base of Red Rock Canyon. It offers upscale living with abundant parks, golf courses, high-end shopping, and resort-style amenities while maintaining a strong community feel.

Learn more about Summerlin →

Las Vegas

The core areas of Las Vegas provide the most urban experience in the valley, with easy access to the Strip, downtown, and major employment centers. It offers a wide variety of housing options and is popular with both first-time buyers and investors.

Learn more about Las Vegas →

Green Valley

Green Valley is one of Henderson’s most established neighborhoods, known for its mature feel, tree-lined streets, and convenient location. It offers a suburban lifestyle with good access to shopping, dining, and parks while remaining close to the Las Vegas Strip.

Learn more about Green Valley →

Inspirada

Inspirada is a newer master-planned community in Henderson that has become very popular with young families. It features modern homes, numerous parks, walking trails, and a strong sense of community with regular events and activities.

Learn more about Inspirada →

Anthem

Anthem is an upscale, established master-planned community in Henderson known for its golf courses and country club lifestyle. It offers a more premium suburban experience with excellent schools and a quieter pace while remaining within easy reach of the Strip.

Learn more about Anthem →

North Las Vegas

North Las Vegas is the most affordable of the major cities in the Las Vegas Valley and has experienced significant growth in recent years. It offers good value for first-time buyers and military families due to its proximity to Nellis Air Force Base.

Learn more about North Las Vegas →

Boulder City

Boulder City is unique in Nevada because gambling is not allowed within city limits. Located about 30–35 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip, it has a historic, small-town atmosphere with a charming downtown and easy access to Lake Mead and outdoor recreation.

Learn more about Boulder City →

Pahrump

Pahrump is located about an hour west of Las Vegas and offers a more rural and affordable alternative for buyers. It has become increasingly popular with retirees and remote workers seeking lower home prices and a quieter lifestyle while still being within driving distance of the Las Vegas Valley.

Learn more about Pahrump →
