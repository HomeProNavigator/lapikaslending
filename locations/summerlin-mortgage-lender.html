Overview of Summerlin

Summerlin is widely recognized as one of the premier master-planned communities in the United States. Located in the western part of the Las Vegas Valley, it sits at the foothills of the Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area and offers a resort-style suburban lifestyle with extensive amenities.

The community features a wide range of housing options, from luxury custom homes to more moderately priced residences. Summerlin is known for its abundant parks, golf courses, walking trails, and high-end shopping and dining options, including the Red Rock Resort and the Downtown Summerlin area.

Many buyers are attracted to Summerlin for its strong sense of community, well-maintained neighborhoods, and convenient access to both outdoor recreation and the broader Las Vegas metropolitan area (typically 20–25 minutes to the Strip).

Neighborhoods in Summerlin

The Ridges & The Summit

Upscale custom and semi-custom home communities with larger lots and premium views. Popular with buyers seeking higher-end properties.

Summerlin South & The Pueblo

Family-friendly sections with a mix of single-family homes, townhomes, and strong community amenities including parks and trails.

Downtown Summerlin

The vibrant core of the community featuring shopping, dining, entertainment, and the Red Rock Resort. Offers a more urban-suburban lifestyle.

Other Areas

Summerlin includes many distinct villages and neighborhoods, each with its own character, amenities, and housing styles ranging from luxury to more moderately priced options.

Real Estate Market in Summerlin

Summerlin has historically maintained strong demand and relatively resilient pricing compared to the broader Las Vegas Valley. The community’s master-planned amenities, proximity to Red Rock Canyon, and high-quality schools contribute to its appeal among move-up buyers and professionals.

The market in Summerlin tends to feature a mix of newer construction and established neighborhoods. Desirable sections with golf course views or proximity to Downtown Summerlin often move faster and command higher prices.

Schools, Parks & Lifestyle

Education & Amenities

Summerlin is served by highly regarded schools and offers extensive community amenities including over 230 parks, numerous trails, golf courses, and the Red Rock Resort. The community is designed with an emphasis on outdoor living and recreation.

Lifestyle

Residents enjoy easy access to Red Rock Canyon for hiking and outdoor activities, along with upscale shopping and dining at Downtown Summerlin. The community maintains a polished, resort-style atmosphere while fostering a strong sense of neighborhood connection.

Location & Accessibility

Summerlin is located approximately 20–25 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip and offers convenient access to major highways. Its western location provides quick access to Red Rock Canyon while still being within a reasonable commute to employment centers throughout the Las Vegas Valley.
