Henderson, Nevada

A well-established suburban city offering a wide range of housing options and strong community amenities in Southern Nevada.

Overview of Henderson

Henderson is the second-largest city in Nevada and one of the most established suburban areas in the Las Vegas Valley. Located just southeast of Las Vegas, it has grown from a small industrial town into a major residential hub with a wide variety of housing styles and master-planned communities.

The city is known for its balance of newer construction and mature neighborhoods, offering buyers options across different price points and lifestyles. Many parts of Henderson feature well-maintained parks, community centers, and convenient access to shopping and dining.

Henderson sits approximately 15–20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip, making it an attractive option for those who want suburban living while still having relatively easy access to employment centers and entertainment.

Neighborhoods in Henderson

Green Valley

One of Henderson’s most established areas, known for its mature feel, tree-lined streets, and convenient location near major shopping and dining options.

Anthem

An upscale master-planned community featuring golf courses, country club amenities, and larger lots in many sections.

Inspirada

A newer master-planned community with modern homes, multiple parks, and a strong emphasis on community events and outdoor spaces.

Other Areas

Henderson also includes a mix of older established neighborhoods and newer developments, giving buyers a broad range of choices in terms of home age, style, and price point.

Real Estate Market in Henderson

Henderson has maintained relatively stable real estate conditions compared to some other parts of the Las Vegas Valley. The city offers a wide spectrum of housing, from more affordable townhomes and condos to larger single-family homes in master-planned communities.

Many buyers are drawn to Henderson for its variety of housing stock and the presence of well-established neighborhoods alongside newer developments. The market tends to attract both local move-up buyers and out-of-state purchasers looking for suburban living with good access to the Las Vegas area.

Schools, Parks & Lifestyle

Education

Henderson is served by the Clark County School District and contains several highly rated schools. Many families are drawn to specific neighborhoods based on school boundaries and the availability of newer or well-maintained school facilities.

Parks & Recreation

The city maintains numerous parks, trails, and recreational facilities. Several master-planned communities within Henderson also include private parks, pools, and community centers for residents.

Location & Accessibility

Henderson offers relatively convenient access to the Las Vegas Strip (typically 15–20 minutes depending on traffic and exact location) and major employment centers. The city is also well-connected via major highways, making commuting to other parts of the valley manageable for many residents.

Henderson’s location makes it a practical choice for those who want suburban amenities while still being within a reasonable drive of downtown Las Vegas and the Strip.
