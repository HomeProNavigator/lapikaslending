Henderson, Nevada

One of Southern Nevada’s largest and most established cities, offering a wide range of housing options and suburban amenities.

Overview of Henderson

Henderson is the second-largest city in Nevada and one of the most established suburban areas in the Las Vegas Valley. Located just southeast of Las Vegas, it has grown into a major residential hub with a wide variety of housing styles, from newer master-planned communities to more established neighborhoods.

The city offers a strong balance of suburban living with convenient access to both the Las Vegas Strip (typically 15–20 minutes) and major employment centers. Many parts of Henderson feature well-maintained parks, community centers, and good access to shopping and dining.

Neighborhoods in Henderson

Green Valley

One of Henderson’s most established areas, known for its mature feel, tree-lined streets, and convenient access to shopping and dining at the Galleria at Sunset area.

Anthem

An upscale master-planned community featuring golf courses, country club amenities, and larger lots in many sections. Offers a more premium suburban experience.

Inspirada

A newer master-planned community with modern homes, multiple parks, walking trails, and a strong community atmosphere with frequent events.

Other Areas

Henderson also includes a mix of older established neighborhoods and newer developments, giving buyers a broad range of housing styles and price points.

Real Estate Market in Henderson

Henderson offers a wide spectrum of housing, from more affordable townhomes and condos to larger single-family homes in master-planned communities. The market has remained relatively stable compared to some other parts of the Las Vegas Valley, with a good balance of newer construction and established neighborhoods.

Many buyers are drawn to Henderson for its variety of housing stock and the presence of well-established communities alongside newer developments. The market tends to attract both local move-up buyers and out-of-state purchasers looking for suburban living with good access to the Las Vegas area.

Schools, Parks & Lifestyle

Education

Henderson is served by the Clark County School District and contains several highly rated schools. Many families consider specific neighborhoods based on school boundaries and the availability of newer or well-maintained facilities.

Parks & Recreation

The city maintains numerous parks, trails, and recreational facilities. Several master-planned communities within Henderson also include private parks, pools, and community centers for residents.

Location & Accessibility

Henderson offers relatively convenient access to the Las Vegas Strip (typically 15–20 minutes depending on traffic and exact location) and major employment centers. The city is well-connected via major highways, making commuting to other parts of the valley manageable for many residents.

Frequently Asked Questions About Henderson

How far is Henderson from the Las Vegas Strip?
Most parts of Henderson are approximately 15–20 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip, depending on traffic and the specific neighborhood.
What types of homes are available in Henderson?
Henderson offers a wide range of housing, including single-family homes in master-planned communities, townhomes, and some condo options across various price points.
Is Henderson a good area for buyers looking for newer homes?
Yes. Several newer master-planned communities, such as Inspirada, have been developed in Henderson over the past 10–15 years, offering more modern construction options.
