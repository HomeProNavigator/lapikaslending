Overview of Anthem

Anthem is an upscale master-planned community located in Henderson. It is known for its golf courses, country club amenities, and well-maintained neighborhoods. The community was developed with a focus on a higher-end suburban lifestyle and has remained popular with buyers seeking premium features and a more established feel.

Anthem offers a range of housing options, including larger single-family homes on spacious lots in many sections. The community includes golf courses, parks, and recreational facilities that contribute to its resort-style atmosphere.

Its location in Henderson provides relatively convenient access to the Las Vegas Strip (approximately 20–25 minutes) while offering a quieter, more upscale suburban environment.

Neighborhoods & Lifestyle in Anthem

Golf & Country Club Lifestyle

Anthem is known for its golf courses and country club amenities. Many residents enjoy access to these facilities, which contribute to the community’s upscale, resort-style atmosphere.

Established Community

Anthem is a well-established master-planned community with mature landscaping and consistent design standards across many neighborhoods. It offers a more premium suburban experience.

Parks & Recreation

The community features parks, trails, and recreational facilities. Residents often highlight the combination of golf, outdoor spaces, and community amenities as a major advantage.

Housing Options

Anthem offers a range of single-family homes, including many on larger lots. The community includes both established sections and some newer development areas.

Real Estate Market in Anthem

Anthem has historically maintained strong demand, particularly in sections with golf course views or proximity to country club amenities. The community tends to attract buyers looking for larger homes and a more upscale suburban lifestyle.

Because it is an established master-planned community, many homes in Anthem feature mature landscaping and consistent community standards. The market in Anthem often moves at a steady pace, with premium properties sometimes commanding higher prices and faster sales.

Schools & Accessibility

Schools

Anthem is served by the Clark County School District and includes several highly regarded schools. School quality is often cited as one of the reasons families choose the community.

Proximity

Anthem is located in Henderson and is approximately 20–25 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip. It offers convenient access to major highways while providing a more upscale suburban environment.
