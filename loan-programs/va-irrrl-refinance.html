What is a VA IRRRL?

The VA Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan (IRRRL), often called a VA Streamline Refinance, is a special program available only to veterans, active-duty service members, and eligible surviving spouses who already have a VA-backed home loan.

Its main purpose is to help you secure a lower interest rate or switch from an adjustable-rate mortgage (ARM) to a more stable fixed-rate loan with significantly less paperwork and hassle than a traditional refinance.

Key Advantages of a VA IRRRL

Minimal Paperwork

No income verification or credit check is typically required. The process is much faster than a standard refinance.

No Appraisal Needed

In most cases, a home appraisal is not required, saving time and money.

Low or No Out-of-Pocket Costs

Closing costs can usually be rolled into the new loan so you don’t need to bring cash to closing.

Lower VA Funding Fee

Only a 0.5% VA funding fee applies — much lower than standard VA loans.
