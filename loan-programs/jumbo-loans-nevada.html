Frequently Asked Questions About Jumbo Loans in Nevada

What is the loan limit for a jumbo loan in Nevada?
Jumbo loans are for amounts above the conforming limit (typically $766,550+ in most Nevada counties in 2026).
What credit score do I need for a jumbo loan?
Most jumbo lenders prefer credit scores of 700 or higher, with 740+ typically receiving the best rates.
How much down payment is required for a jumbo loan in Nevada?
Most jumbo loans require at least 10–20% down, though some programs may allow less with strong qualifications.
Can I use a jumbo loan to refinance in Nevada?
Yes. Many Nevada homeowners refinance higher-value properties using jumbo loans to secure better rates or terms.
