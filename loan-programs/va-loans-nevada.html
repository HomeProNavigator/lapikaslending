VA Loan Qualification Requirements in Nevada

Basic Requirements

  • Valid Certificate of Eligibility (COE)
  • Satisfactory credit history (no strict minimum score)
  • Stable income and employment
  • Debt-to-income ratio typically under 41%

Frequently Asked Questions About VA Loans in Nevada

Do I need a down payment for a VA loan in Nevada?
No. VA loans allow 100% financing for eligible veterans and service members.
What is the VA funding fee?
The VA funding fee is a one-time fee that helps fund the VA loan program. It can usually be financed into the loan and varies based on your service history and whether you're using the benefit for the first time.
Can I use a VA loan to refinance my current mortgage in Nevada?
Yes. You can use a VA Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan (IRRRL) or a cash-out refinance depending on your goals.
How long does it take to close on a VA loan in Las Vegas or Henderson?
Most VA transactions in Nevada close within 30–45 days once the offer is accepted, though this can vary.
