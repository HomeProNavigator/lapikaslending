Who First-Time Buyer Programs Are Best For in Nevada

First-time home buyer programs in Nevada are designed for individuals and families purchasing their first primary residence. This includes people who have never owned a home, as well as those who haven’t owned a home in the last three years. These programs are especially helpful for younger buyers, growing families, and professionals moving to the Las Vegas Valley.

Qualification Requirements for First-Time Buyers in Nevada

Common Requirements

  • First-time buyer status (or haven’t owned a home in 3 years)
  • Stable income and employment
  • Reasonable credit history
  • Completion of homebuyer education (often required)
  • Debt-to-income ratio within lender guidelines
