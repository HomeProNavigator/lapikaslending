Refinance Loans in Nevada

Lower your rate, shorten your term, or access your home equity with expert guidance from Derek Lapikas.

Rate & Term Refinance

Rate and term refinancing is the most common type of refinance. It allows you to secure a lower interest rate or change the length of your loan without taking cash out of your home.

Benefits

  • Lower monthly payments
  • Reduce total interest paid
  • Shorten your loan term
  • Switch from ARM to fixed rate

Considerations

  • Closing costs apply
  • Need sufficient equity
  • Rate must be low enough to justify costs

Cash-Out Refinance

Cash-out refinancing lets you replace your current mortgage with a larger loan and receive the difference in cash. This is useful for home improvements, debt consolidation, or major expenses.

Benefits

  • Access home equity as cash
  • Fund renovations or major purchases
  • Consolidate high-interest debt
  • Potentially lower overall interest rate

Considerations

  • Increases your loan balance
  • Higher monthly payment possible
  • More equity required
